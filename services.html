Sugar

At TQN Industries, we recognize sugar as more than just a sweetener — it’s a symbol of tradition and celebration in every Indian household. From everyday moments to festive occasions, sugar plays an essential role, making sweet experiences even more memorable and culturally significant.

Sugar is derived from sugarcane, cultivated across the fertile regions of North, West, and South India. TQN Industries operates multiple advanced sugar processing units in major sugarcane-growing belts including Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka. The harvested cane is processed to extract juice, which is then refined into high-quality sugar crystals. Our sugar not only meets the domestic demand efficiently but is also exported to various international markets, reflecting our global commitment to quality.
